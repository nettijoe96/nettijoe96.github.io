Peach Picking

We wrap ourselves In light linen coats Grey woolen socks And leather shoes To journey With baskets in hand To narrow streets Of yellow peach trees That meander through the wood We follow the trail up a hill To feel the sky widen Before us To dance freely As it does To pause. To stitch together Shapes, lines, colors Where trees become forest Leaves--a canopy Dancing swiftly, swiftly Down blades of grass Through matchbox meadows Patchwork paths We are free. Weaving in and out Of expansive fabrics Crimson bows Against the horizon Tapping toes On the rising sun
